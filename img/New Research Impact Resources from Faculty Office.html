New Research Impact Resources from Faculty Office

Dear Professors,

What if your theory can solve one of society’s most “practical problems”? Research impact is more than just a metric. It is “the good that researchers can do in the world.”

To help you bridge the gap between academic excellence and real-world change, the Faculty Office has launched two new resources.
🌐 “Research Impact” Webpage

Our public-facing website has been redesigned to highlight our Faculty’s meaningful contributions beyond academia.

  • Get Inspired: Browse selected impact cases and see how colleagues are influencing policy, population, and social welfare issues.
  • Follow the Framework: Learn the five-step pathway to plan, engage, and evaluate your project’s reach.

Access here: https://socsc.cuhk.edu.hk/research/research-impact/
📂 Information Pack and Toolkit

Our new suite provides a comprehensive guide to operationalising your impact-related success.

  • Identify Funding: Use the “Selecting the Right Grant” flowchart to find the perfect scheme.
  • Maximise Opportunities: Reference our calendar to stay current on internal and external grant deadlines.
  • Plan, Track, and Evidence: Leverage our templates to map stakeholder engagement and gather the right data for your project.

Access here: Information Pack and Toolkit

Need a Spark of Inspiration?

The Social Innovation Team at KTO offers year-round Impact Consultations to help refine your ideas and connect you with partners. Book a session: https://kto.cuhk.edu.hk/en/news-events/events/others/year-round-impact-consultation

Reminder: Strategic Impact Enhancement Fund (SIEF) (2025-26)

The SIEF 2025-26, established by the Research Committee to sustain institutional research impact development at CUHK, is now open for applications (Faculty internal deadline: 4 June 2026). Please refer to our previous email for details.

We hope these resources empower your journey beyond.
